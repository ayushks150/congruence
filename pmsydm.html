Pradhan Mantri Shram Yogi Maan-Dhan Yojana (PM-SYM)

About the Scheme

The Pradhan Mantri Shram Yogi Maan-Dhan Yojana (PM-SYM) is a social security scheme initiated by the Government of India to provide old age protection and financial security to unorganized workers. It is a voluntary and contributory pension scheme.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Unorganized workers in the age group of 18 to 40 years.
  • Should have a monthly income of less than Rs. 15,000.
  • Should not be covered under any other social security scheme.

Application Process

  1. Visit the official website of PM-SYM.
  2. Download and fill the application form.
  3. Submit the form along with required documents to the nearest Common Service Centre (CSC) or Pension Implementing Agency (PIA).
  4. Pay the initial contribution amount as specified.
  5. Receive the Shram Yogi Maan-Dhan card.
